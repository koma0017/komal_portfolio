WHO I AM ?


Welcome to my mini Portfolio. I'm Komal Kochar, A girl passionate about design. I am Interactive Media Design student at Algonquin College, Ottawa. Basically, I am from India. I love art, illustration, and design. One of my dreams is to be a reputed Graphic Designer and now I am working towards it.

I love to draw since childhood and this hobby leads me to into the design field. I completed Multimedia and Animation Ceritification course in 2015 and started working as a freelancer. After one year got opportunity to work abroad and here I am.
